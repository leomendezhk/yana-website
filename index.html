The booking process

1

Take a look at the FAQ'S & Pricing in the Info page. After that, fill out the Contact Form and we'll get to chatting! I'll send you a detailed pricing guide and we'll make sure we're a great fit for each other!

2

Once we're ready to lock in your date, I'll send you the booking emails with info on locations, a meeting time, and a wedding guide or outfit suggestions. You'll get the contract signed, invoice paid & questionnaire all filled out and we'll be all set!

3

Together, we'll make your dream shoot or Big Day happen! You'll walk away with the most fun, authentic experience and beautiful effortless photos to help you relive the day! We'll keep in touch and remain good friends long after!
