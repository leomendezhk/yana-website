About

Yana Economu is a Cyprus based photographer. Combining her fine art photography and modelling studies, along with a skilled and sensitive eye for portraiture, her images have garnered international acclaim for their honesty, vulnerability, and unfeigned connection to its subjects. Well-traveled and always up for an adventure, she has captured moments and celebrations from around the world. From London to Japan, from France to Hong kong, she is always bringing a unique editorial, aesthetic and artistic sensibility to the photography world. With over 10 years of professional experience, Yana's photographic journey has assumed a rich and varied path. Her work has been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, Brides, C Magazine, Prestige magazine and other international publications and media outlets.

Yana: “I photograph many stunning projects every year, however, I approach every session in the same way, with the belief that I am there to tell each person's unique, beautiful story. For me, capturing once in a lifetime emotions drives me forward as a photographer.”
