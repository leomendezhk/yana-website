A little bit about me

Yana is a Cyprus based photographer, but grew up in a small remote town in Russia . Combining her fine art photography & modelling studies, along with a skilled and sensitive eye for portraiture, her images garnered acclaim for their honesty, vulnerability, and unfeigned connection to its subjects. Well traveled and always up for an adventure, she has photographed celebrations across the world, from Europe to Asia, from Frace to Hong kong and beyond, always bringing a unique editorial and artistic sensibility to the wedding world. With over 10 years of professional experience, Yana's photographic journey has assumed a rich and varied path. Her work has been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, Brides, Martha Stewart Weddings, C Magazine and more.
Yana: “I photograph many stunning projects every year, however, I approach every session in the same way, with the belief that I am there to tell each person unique, beautiful story. For me, capturing once in a lifetime emotions drives me forward as a photographer.”
